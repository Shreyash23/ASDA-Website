Akash Manjunath

Having trained in freestyle for 8 years , Akash's moves are but fun to watch and leant. Having learnt Hip Hop and Salsa for two years under Sai and Kirthi, Akash has not only refined himself, but has become a 'performer' as well.

"From childhood I could move a little better than the other kids in the neighbourhood" says Akash with a modest smile, "It has always been you could say more than a passion, and bordering on an obsession with me I always loved to be on stage and dance and quite enjoyed the limelight that it brings- And this borderline quirk has but led him to being a reputed Instructor .
Sitting in on Akash's class you can see the teacher and performer in him come out. With a charming serene smile, he makes the most complicated routines look like child's play and there is one but intriguing quality about him, watching him dance, the most stubborn NON-DANCERS will want to shake a leg!!

Achievements

Akash has many laurels to his name-

  • All India national Salsa Champion 2011, World Salsa Championship Semi-Finalist.
  • Represented India at the world salsa championship 2012 in Hong Kong with a ranking of no 8.
  • Youngest and only the 3rd couple ever to represent India at the world salsa Championship.
  • Regional finalist in the reality show "Aajajhoom Le" on zoom tv. Invited to perform and teach at the prestigious China Salsa Congress 2011.
  • Performed at the India International Salsa congress 2009 and 2010 and in India hip-hop and Latino festival.
  • Choreographed for many colleges out of which most have won competitions.
  • Choreographed for various celebrities.
  • Choreographed numerous dance routines for corporates including choreography for Deutsche bank's annual event, ENCORE 2012.
  • Senior dance instructor at ASDA and Inventure academy.
