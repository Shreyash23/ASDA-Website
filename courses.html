DANCE ACTIVE

Dance Active is a unique movement education program for children. It combines various dance styles like Jazz, Hip-Hop, Contemporary & Bollywood.

This program also includes the basics of Gymnastics and a new method developed by Ansul called Active Play, in which the use of "fun" equipment increases strength and stability in children.
To put it in simply , Dance Active is 360 degree approach to enhance personality development through dance and performance.
What makes dance active a unique dance programme is the fun filled approach in which the kids are taught to dance using different colourful equipments and dance games. It is a program designed to give structured dance education in a very creative and fun filled way.

Advanced Dance Development Programme

This is newly introduced program in which entries are taken after a round of audition.
In this program, we focus on Contemporary ,Hip-Hop & Bboying.
It not only ensures the growth of a child’s dancing skills but takes them to the basics and roots of dance and creates a vast platform of learning.

Dance Addictive

A new set of classes introduced on public demand, for adults only. which is designed specially for adults which focuses on various styles of dance such as Hip Hop, Contemporary, Salsa & Bollywood.
It is a combination of Dance and Fitness through fun!
