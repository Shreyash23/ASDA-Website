Instructors at ASDA

Ansul Kumar Garg

ASDA is Ansul Kumar Garg's brain child and has been overly successful since it started in 2007. Ansul is a trained dancer is various dance styles such as Contemporary, Hip-Hop, Bboying, Gymnastics, Freestyle, and Bollywood to name a few. He has worked with numerous dance schools across the city and has performed all over the world.
He has choreographed various school productions for Inventure Academy, Kara 4 Kids, Indus International name a few.Ansul is a certified Monkeynastics trainer, a program which is a combination of dance and gymnastics for children from South Africa. Read more...

Swapoorna Sengupta

An Odissi and contemporary dancer Swapoorna Sengupta started her dancing career started at an age of 3 years under GURU Mayadhar Routh . She also is a disciple of Dr. Maya Rao and has graduated as a choreographer from Natya institute of Kathak and Choreography, where she learnt different styles like Indian contemporary, martial arts, folk dances of India, theatre forms techniques of mime & music, stage design and lighting, costume designing & dance drama productions.
Apart from these she is also trained in few western dance styles and has worked under Ashley Lobo from Danceworx, Fernando, Shiamak Dawar, Terence Lewis and Remo D’Souza. Swaporna, she still continues to learn the art of teaching kids dance from ASDA, where she works as an instructor & admin, and teaching little hearts the joy of dance.

Ankita Girish

Ankita Girish, our instructor and administrative person is a qualified Chereographer. She has completed her Graduation in Dance and Choreography from Bangalore Universisty. She has recently completed her Diploma in Dance from ATTAKALARI.
Ankita finds her passion in teaching and training kids and has been following her dream of teaching past five years. Her dream is to spread happiness and joy to the younger ones through dancing.

Akash Manjunath

Having trained in freestyle for 8 years , Akash's moves are but fun to watch and leant. Having learnt Hip Hop and Salsa for two years under Sai and Kirthi, Akash has not only refined himself, but has become a 'performer' as well.
"From childhood I could move a little better than the other kids in the neighbourhood" says Akash with a modest smile. "It has always been you could say more than a passion, and bordering on an obsession with me I always loved to be on stage and dance and quite enjoyed the limelight that it brings"- And this borderline quirk has but led him to being a reputed Instructor .
